
# 🛸 UFO Sightings Analysis

> Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on UFO sightings across the United States  
> over the last century — including correlations with US general elections.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a dataset of **80,000+ UFO sighting reports** collected 
from the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC). The goal is to uncover 
patterns in where, when, and how UFOs are reported across the United States.

---

## ❓ Questions Answered

1. 🔵 What is the most common **UFO shape** reported?
2. 📍 Which **US state** has the most UFO sightings?
3. 👥 Which states have the most sightings **per capita**?
4. 📅 Which **year** had the highest number of sightings?
5. 🗓️ Which **month** sees the most UFO reports?
6. 💬 What do witnesses **say most**? (Word Cloud)
7. 🗳️ Is there a **correlation** with US general elections?

---

## 📊 Dataset

- **Source:** [Kaggle - UFO Sightings](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/NUFORC/ufo-sightings)
- **Records:** 80,000+ sighting reports
- **Features:** datetime, city, state, country, shape, duration, comments, lat/lon

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Libraries

| Library | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `pandas` | Data loading & manipulation |
| `matplotlib` | Static visualizations |
| `seaborn` | Statistical plots |
| `plotly` | Interactive maps & charts |
| `wordcloud` | Witness comments visualization |

---

## 📈 Key Findings

- **Light** is the most commonly reported UFO shape
- **California** leads all US states in total sightings
- Sightings peak in **July** — around Independence Day
- Noticeable spike in sightings around **election years**
